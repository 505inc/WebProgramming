Introduction

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG developed by Chinese studio Game Science, based on the classic Chinese novel Journey to the West.

The game focuses on the legendary Monkey King, Sun Wukong, as he embarks on a journey filled with mythological creatures, intense combat, and stunning visuals brought to life by Unreal Engine 5.

Release date: 2024 (TBD)
  • Developer: Game Science
  • Genre: Action RPG
    • What is this game about?

    The game is a retelling of the famous Chinese story Journey to the West, with a focus on Sun Wukong's quest for immortality and freedom.

    Players will control Wukong as he battles mythical enemies, learns new abilities, and overcomes challenges inspired by ancient Chinese folklore.

    The game mixes fluid combat, stealth, and shape-shifting mechanics to offer diverse gameplay experiences.
  • Set in a dark, atmospheric version of ancient China.
    • Main mechanics

    The core gameplay of Black Myth: Wukong involves dynamic and fast-paced combat. Wukong can use his staff to attack enemies, perform acrobatic movements, and unleash powerful abilities.

    One of the key mechanics is Wukong's ability to shape-shift into different forms, allowing him to adapt to different situations and enemy types.

    Shape-shifting into creatures: wolves, insects, and more.
  • Combat relies heavily on mastering parries, dodges, and timing attacks.
    • Main characters

    Sun Wukong: The protagonist, known as the Monkey King, who possesses incredible strength, agility, and the ability to transform.

    The Buddha: A mysterious figure in the story, who plays a central role in the narrative, as Wukong seeks to break free from his control.

    Other mythological beings: Dragons, demons, and gods inspired by Chinese mythology.
  • Wukong encounters many mythical creatures as bosses.
    • References

    The game draws heavy inspiration from the 16th-century Chinese novel Journey to the West.

    It also utilizes modern technology, such as Unreal Engine 5, to push graphical boundaries and deliver a cinematic experience in gaming.

    Graphical fidelity powered by NVIDIA DLSS and ray tracing technology.
  • Developers aimed to blend traditional Chinese culture with modern gaming technology.
